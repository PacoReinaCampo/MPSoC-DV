# PROGRAMAS, PROYECTOS Y RAMAS

Los programas de la organización QueenField son:

$$PROGRAM = [TARGET, ACCELERATOR, ENVIRONMENT, ARCHITECTURE, PERIPHERAL, EDA]$$

$$COMPONENT = [MPSoC, SoC, PU]$$

$$UNIT = [MSP430, OR1K, RISCV]$$
